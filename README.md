# Austria Gambling Regulations

This repository contains gambling regulations and regulatory guidelines issued by the **Austria** gambling regulatory authority, converted to Markdown for easy reference and version tracking.

## Documents

| Document | Description |
|---|---|
| [Austria Brief Info En](austria-brief-info-en.md) | Brief information on a ministerial draft concerning the strengthening of player protection, combating illegal gambling, and financing player protection and market observation in Austria. |
| [Austria Cover Letter En](austria-cover-letter-en.md) | Cover letter for the ministerial draft concerning the Gambling Reform Act 2026 and amendments to the Gambling Act and the Telecommunications Act 2021. |
| [Austria Cover Sheet En](austria-cover-sheet-en.md) | Ministerial draft leaflet and impact assessment for the Gambling Reform Act 2026, amending the Gambling Act and the Telecommunications Act 2021. |
| [Austria Explanations En](austria-explanations-en.md) | Explanatory notes to a ministerial draft amending the Austrian Gambling Act, covering online gambling reform, player protection, supervision, and licensing. |
| [Austria Legal Text En](austria-legal-text-en.md) | Ministerial draft text of a Federal Act amending the Gambling Act and the Telecommunications Act 2021. |

## About

The documents in this repository cover licensing requirements, technical standards, and compliance obligations for gambling operators in Austria.

All documents converted from PDF/Word to Markdown format for accessibility and version control purposes.
